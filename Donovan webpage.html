About Me

I am Caroline Donovan, a molecular and cellular biologist with over 6 years of experience in the industry working in drug discovery for small and large pharmaceutical companies. My interests lie in target discovery and validation and in assessing different modalities to treat diseases with unmet need. I identify appropriate cellular disease models to accomplish in vitro proof of concept studies.
